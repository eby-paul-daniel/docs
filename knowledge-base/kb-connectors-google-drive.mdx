## Connecting to Google Drive
Follow these steps to set up a Google Drive connection to the Knowledge Base:
1. In your EKB project, navigate to the **Knowledge Base**.
2. At the top-right corner, click the **gear** icon to access the Knowledge Base Settings.
3. Navigate to the **Connectors** tab.
4. Find the _Google Drive_ option and click **Connect**. You'll be redirected to Google's login page.
5. Authenticate into your Google account and provide all the necessary permissions for EKB to access your Google account.
6. Once connected, you should see your Google account listed under the Google Drive connection.<br/>![Google Drive Connected](/img/knowledge-base/google-drive-connected.png)

<Check>Now you're ready to sync files from Google Drive into the Knowledge Base!</Check>
## Adding Data from Google Drive
1. Navigate to **Knowledge Base** > **KB Data**.
2. Click **+ New** and select **URL Upload**.
3. In the pop-up that appears, paste the URL of the Google Drive file or folder you want to add to the Knowledge Base and click **Add URL**.

<Check>Once files are added, you'll receive the following confirmation message:<br/>![Confirmation Message](/img/knowledge-base/add-url-confirmation-message.png)</Check>
