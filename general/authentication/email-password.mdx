In this article, you will learn how to authenticate your EKB account using the traditional email and password method. This straightforward process allows you to create your account, sign in, manage your password, and ensure your account's security. By following the steps outlined below, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of how to effectively use email/password authentication.

## Email/Password Authentication

Email/password authentication is a widely used method that enables users to create accounts and sign in using their email address and a secure password. This method is standard for most users and provides a reliable way to access your EKB account.

### Signing Up

To begin using EKB, you must first create an account. The following steps will guide you through the sign-up process:

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**  
   Start by clicking **Sign Up** on the login page or visiting the sign-up page directly.

2. **Enter Your Information**  
   You will need to provide your email address, create a secure password, and confirm your password if required.

3. **Complete Sign-Up**  
   Click **Sign Up** or **Create Account**, verify your email address if necessary, and complete any additional setup steps.

### Signing In

Once you have created your account, you can sign in using the following steps:

1. **Navigate to Login**  
   Go to the EKB login page and enter your email address and password.

2. **Authenticate**  
   Click **Sign In** or **Log In**. After successful authentication, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

### Password Requirements

EKB enforces specific password security requirements to protect your account:

- **Minimum Length**: Passwords must meet minimum length requirements.
- **Complexity**: Passwords may include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
- **Security**: Passwords are securely hashed and salted.
- **Storage**: Passwords are never stored in plain text.

### Password Reset

If you forget your password, follow these steps to reset it:

1. **Request Password Reset**  
   Click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your email address, and click **Send Reset Link**.

2. **Check Your Email**  
   Look for the password reset email and click the reset link provided.

3. **Create New Password**  
   Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Reset Password**.

4. **Sign In**  
   You can now use your new password to sign in.

### Account Security

To maintain the security of your account, consider these best practices:

- Use a strong, unique password.
- Do not share your password with anyone.
- Change your password regularly.
- Use different passwords for different services.
- Enable two-factor authentication if available.
