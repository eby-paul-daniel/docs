This article will guide you through the process of authenticating your EKB account using Google Sign-In. This method allows for a more convenient and secure way to access your account without the need to remember a separate password. You will learn how to sign in with your Google account, the benefits of using Google Sign-In, and troubleshooting tips for common issues.

Google Sign-In enables users to authenticate using their existing Google account, which simplifies the login process by eliminating the need for a separate password for EKB. This method leverages Google's security features to provide a secure and efficient sign-in experience.

### Sign-In With Google
To sign in with your Google account, follow these steps:

1. **Click Sign in with Google**  
   On the login page, click **Sign in with Google** or the Google Sign-In button.

2. **Select Google Account**  
   Choose your Google account from the list or enter your Google email address.

3. **Grant Permissions**  
   Review the permissions requested by EKB and click **Allow** or **Continue** to grant access.

4. **Automatic Sign-In**  
   You will be automatically signed in to EKB and redirected to your dashboard.

### Benefits
Using Google Sign-In offers several advantages:

- **No Password Required**: You can use your existing Google account to log in.
- **Quick Access**: This method provides a faster sign-in process.
- **Secure**: It leverages Google's robust security infrastructure.
- **Single Account**: One Google account can be used for multiple services.
- **Automatic Updates**: Your profile information is synced from Google.

### Requirements
To use Google Sign-In, you must meet the following requirements:

- **Google Account**: A valid Google account is necessary.
- **Internet Connection**: An active internet connection is required for authentication.
- **Browser Support**: Ensure you are using a modern browser with JavaScript enabled.
- **Permissions**: You must grant permission to use Google Sign-In.

### First-Time Setup
When signing in with Google for the first time, you may need to complete the following steps:

1. **Account Creation**  
   EKB will automatically create an account using your Google email, syncing your profile information from Google.

2. **Profile Completion**  
   You may be prompted to complete additional profile information and configure some settings.

3. **Access Granted**  
   After setup, you will have full access to all features of EKB.

### Managing Google Sign-In

**Linking Accounts**: If you already have an email/password account, you can link it to Google Sign-In, allowing you to use either method.

**Unlinking Accounts**: If you wish to unlink your Google account, you will need to set up email/password authentication first.

### Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, consider the following common problems and solutions:

**Problem**: Google Sign-In button not appearing  
**Solution**: Check your internet connection, clear browser cache and cookies, try a different browser, disable extensions that might block it, and ensure JavaScript is enabled.

**Problem**: Permission denied or error during sign-in  
**Solution**: Verify you are using a valid Google account, ensure your Google account is active, try signing out and back in, and check for pop-up blockers.

**Problem**: Account not created after Google Sign-In  
**Solution**: Verify your email address, check if an account already exists, try signing in with email/password if applicable, and contact [Support](mailto:support@getodin.ai) for assistance.

For additional information on Google Sign-In, you may find the official Google documentation helpful: [Google Sign-In Documentation](https://developers.google.com/identity/sign-in/web/sign-in).
